Projects

Project Pioneer

During the summer before my freshman year in college, I attended Google CSSI. Project Pioneer, the result of a three-person collaboration, was my final project for the program.

It is a website aimed to help incoming, and specifically first-generation, college students find all the information they need regarding the college application process. My job was to develop a forums page as well as to maintain the deployment and database via Google App Engine and Datastore.
